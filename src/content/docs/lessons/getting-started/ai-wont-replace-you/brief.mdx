## What you'll learn

This is the first lesson on Clawdemy, and it's the mission lesson. It answers the question you probably had when you arrived: what happens to me when AI gets good at my job? The short version is that AI doesn't come for your job the way the headlines suggest. It comes for the parts of your work you can describe to a machine, and it amplifies whatever you bring to the rest. This lesson is about what "the rest" actually is, and how to find yours.

## Before you start

**Prerequisites:** none. You don't need any technical background, and you don't need Clawless installed yet. The worked exercise is pen and paper.

## By the end, you'll be able to

- Explain what "human delta" means and why AI amplifies it rather than replacing it
- Distinguish the mechanical parts of your work from the judgment parts
- Identify one specific task from your week where AI would give you real leverage

## Time and difficulty

- **Read time:** about 12 minutes
- **Practice time:** about 10 minutes (a short audit on paper)
- **Difficulty:** intro
