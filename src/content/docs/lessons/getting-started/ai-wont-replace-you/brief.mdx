## What you'll learn

You'll come away understanding why AI tools are best understood as collaborators that change what's valuable about your work, not as competitors that replace you. By the end of this lesson, you'll have a concrete frame for identifying where AI helps you compound your impact, and a specific first step you can take this week.

## Before you start

**Prerequisites:** none. This is the first lesson on Clawdemy.

## By the end, you'll be able to

- Identify the three categories of work AI augments well today
- Distinguish your "human delta" from the parts of your job AI can do
- Name one task to start delegating to an AI agent this week
