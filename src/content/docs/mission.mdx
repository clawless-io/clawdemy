## Turn fear into fluency

Clawdemy is the sister project to [Clawless Computer](https://clawless.ai). Clawless gives people a tool to use AI without the terminal. Clawdemy teaches them what to *do* with that tool, and more broadly, what to do with the AI revolution they keep reading about.

Most AI education is written by people who already love AI, *for* people who already love AI. The person who's worried about their job bounces. That person is who we write for.

## Two halves of one mission

| Clawless | Clawdemy |
|---|---|
| Closed-source desktop AI app | Free, open educational platform |
| Solves the *tool* problem | Solves the *literacy* problem |
| Makes AI accessible without code | Makes AI accessible without fear |

A Clawless install and a Clawdemy lesson are meant to work together. You can use either alone. You get more from both.

## What we optimize for

**Primary outcome:** a non-technical reader finishes their first Clawdemy track and *feels less afraid and more capable*. Concretely, they've built or run something themselves.

Secondary outcome: confident readers become confident Clawless users. Every published lesson is a funnel into the product, but only a soft one. Every lesson also stands on its own.

## What we won't do

- No ads, ever.
- No paywall, ever.
- No "premium" tier.
- No popups asking you to support us.
- No dark patterns.

If Clawdemy ever feels like any of those, we've failed our own mission. Please tell us.

## How lessons are made

Clawdemy is built by a small team using an agentic content pipeline. Source material comes from open educational sources (Stanford lectures, public documentation, community discussions), gets distilled into our six-artifact lesson format, and is human-reviewed before anything ships. Lesson content is published under [CC BY-SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/) so the work compounds in the open.

Operations, planning, and the specifics of how our agents are configured stay internal. The lessons are the product. The workshop floor isn't.

## Who's behind Clawdemy

Built by **[Jay Siddiqi](https://www.linkedin.com/in/junaidsiddiqi/)**, a developer on a mission to make AI useful for everyone, not just engineers.

Clawdemy teaches people how to actually use AI in the real world. It's the education layer for [Clawless](https://clawless.ai), the desktop OS that turns OpenClaw's powerful agent engine into something anyone can run.

We believe AI should make people more powerful, not obsolete, so our lessons focus on turning fear into fluency. Articles, walkthroughs and practical guides show you the "why" behind AI and then bring those skills to life in Clawless. No command line, no YAML, just real-world impact.

Lessons are accelerated by our own agentic pipeline, but nothing is published without a human review, so you can trust the quality.
