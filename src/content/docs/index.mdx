## What Clawdemy is

Clawdemy is free, public AI education for the people who are most worried that AI will replace them. Articles, walkthroughs, and practical guides for turning fear into fluency.

We believe anyone can become genuinely powerful with AI. Not a passive user of someone else's assistant, but someone who composes, automates, and owns their own workflows.
